### Takeaways From Expo Presentation
I was number 14 to present in the expo and didn't go right away, so at first when visitors were taking a look at my project I took my time going through each slide and thoroughly explaining each visual aspect that I wanted to use in order for people to grasp the overall functionality while still showing enough of the code. 

When the actual judges came around, they set a timer for 1 minute and 40 seconds, but I didn't really have an elevaror pitch in mind so I basically ended up speeding through about 3/4 of my presentation, having to skip ahead throughout my video demo as well to meet the limited time-constrainsts. But I know that the judges still were able to clearly see and understand the functionality of my product right away when they saw the login feature and I skipped ahead throughout the create, delete, update and logout functionalities that I had time to showcase, since they gave me some verbal praise for my app while I was presenting the demo.
